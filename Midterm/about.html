Meet the Bubs
The Tufts Beelzebubs is a renowned a cappella group from Tufts University in Medford, Massachusetts. Founded in 1962, we've been making music and performing across the country and the world ever since. Our group is made up of Tufts students from various majors and backgrounds, united by our love of music and our commitment to excellence in performance. We've won numerous awards and been featured on TV shows like "The Sing-Off" and "Glee."
By purchasing merchandise from our store, you're directly supporting the Tufts Beelzebubs and helping us continue to make music and perform. All proceeds from our sales go towards funding our group's activities, including recording albums, arranging music, and traveling to competitions and gigs. We're grateful for the support of our fans and the opportunity to share our music with the world.
Our Mechandise
We're proud to offer a range of merchandise that showcases our group's unique style and personality. Choose from our selection of t-shirts, sweatshirts, and hats. We've got something for every Beelzebub fan. Our designs feature our signature red and white color scheme, as well as our iconic devil logo. Each piece is carefully crafted with attention to detail, ensuring that it meets our high standards of quality. Whether you're a Tufts alum, a current student, or just a fan of a cappella music, our merch is a great way to show your support for the Beelzebubs and look good doing it.
